
## Commands to record the waypoint
After moving ROSBot XL through teleop keys use either one of these commands to record the pose.
ros2 topic echo /odometry/filtered --field pose.pose --once
ros2 topic echo /rosbot_xl_base_controller/odom --field pose.pose --once
